Chocolate Chip Cookies

These chewy, gooey chocolate chip cookies are the perfect treat for any occasion. Easy to make and always a crowd-pleaser!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Add eggs and vanilla extract, mix well.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture.
  6. Stir in the chocolate chips evenly.
  7. Scoop spoonfuls of dough onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until golden brown.
